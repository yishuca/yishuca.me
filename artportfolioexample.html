About Me

The artist behind the creations

Hello! I'm Elena, a passionate visual artist with over 10 years of experience in creating captivating artworks across various mediums. My journey in art began when I was just a child, and it has evolved into a lifelong pursuit of creative expression.

I specialize in both digital and traditional mediums, with a particular focus on creating pieces that evoke emotion and tell stories. My work has been featured in galleries across the country and in numerous private collections.
