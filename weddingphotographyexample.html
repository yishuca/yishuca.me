About Me

Hello, I'm Sarah, a passionate wedding photographer with over 10 years of experience capturing love stories. My approach blends photojournalism with fine art to create images that are both authentic and breathtaking.

I believe every couple has a unique story to tell, and my goal is to document your special day in a way that reflects your personality and the emotions of the moment. From the nervous excitement of getting ready to the joyful celebration, I'll be there to preserve every precious memory.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me hiking with my golden retriever or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen.
